# Content Pillars

## Pillar 1: CS2 Shorts

Priority: main growth engine.

Best clip types:

- toxic teammates
- solo queue chaos
- team-kill attempts
- enemy timing mistakes
- reload panic
- multi-kill chains
- clean reaction kills
- funny FPS fails
- "I was already waiting" moments
- "They pushed one by one" moments

Hook examples:

- MY TEAM TRIED TO KILL ME 💀
- HE PEEKED TOO LATE
- SOLO QUEUE IS PAIN
- NO TIME TO RELOAD
- THEY PUSHED ONE BY ONE

Title examples:

- My Team Tried to Kill Me 💀 | CS2
- He Peeked at the Wrong Time 💀 | CS2
- They Pushed One by One 💀 | CS2
- Solo Queue Is Pain 💀 | CS2

Hashtag examples:

- `#CS2`
- `#CounterStrike2`
- `#CS2Shorts`
- `#SoloQueue`
- `#FPSGaming`
- `#GamingShorts`

Avoid:

- long pre-round setup
- scoreboard-only clips
- slow walking
- titles that only describe mechanics
- text over crosshair, enemy, or kill feed

## Pillar 2: Survival Horror / Zombie Games

Games:

- 7 Days to Die
- Metro
- Resident Evil

Best clip types:

- zombie panic
- horde attacks
- base breach
- survival horror tension
- mutant ambush
- jumpscare
- no ammo / no filter / no escape moments
- claustrophobic tunnel moments
- horror clips with fast payoff

Hook examples:

- HORDE NIGHT WENT WRONG
- THE SCREAMER FOUND ME 💀
- THIS TUNNEL WAS TOO QUIET
- NO AMMO LEFT
- I SHOULD NOT HAVE OPENED THAT DOOR

Title examples:

- Zombies Broke Through 💀 | 7 Days to Die
- This Tunnel Was Too Quiet 💀 | Metro
- No Ammo, No Escape 💀 | Resident Evil

Hashtag examples:

- `#SurvivalHorror`
- `#ZombieGame`
- `#HorrorGaming`
- `#ResidentEvil`
- `#7DaysToDie`
- `#GamingShorts`

Avoid:

- inventory management
- long walking with no threat
- unclear dark footage
- clips where the monster/payoff appears too late

## Pillar 3: Minecraft Clips

Important wording: use "Minecraft clips" generally. Do not say "modded Minecraft" unless the footage is specifically modded.

Best clip types:

- creeper timing
- survival fails
- base problems
- unexpected mobs
- funny deaths
- rare discoveries
- Nether / cave panic
- building or survival chaos

Hook examples:

- CREEPER TIMING WAS ILLEGAL 💀
- MY BASE WAS NOT SAFE
- I SHOULD NOT HAVE DUG THERE
- ONE BLOCK AWAY FROM DEATH

Title examples:

- Creeper Timing Was Illegal 💀 | Minecraft
- My Base Was Not Safe | Minecraft
- This Cave Was a Bad Idea 💀 | Minecraft

Hashtag examples:

- `#Minecraft`
- `#MinecraftShorts`
- `#MinecraftClips`
- `#SurvivalMinecraft`
- `#GamingShorts`

Avoid:

- calling clips modded without proof
- long mining with no payoff
- slow building clips unless the final reveal is fast
- overusing generic Minecraft tags unrelated to the clip

## Pillar 4: Cinematic / Action Games

Games:

- RDR2
- GTA RP
- James Bond / 007

Best clip types:

- cinematic close calls
- train jumps
- crashes
- clean landings
- fast takedowns
- suspicious RP meetings
- action scenes that are instantly understandable

Hook examples:

- ARTHUR BARELY MADE IT 💀
- ALMOST FLIPPED BUT SAVED IT
- BOND REACTED INSTANTLY
- THIS RP MEETING FELT SUSPICIOUS

Title examples:

- Arthur Barely Made It 💀 | RDR2
- Almost Flipped but Saved It 💀 | GTA RP
- Bond Reacted Instantly 🔥 | 007 First Light

Hashtag examples:

- `#RDR2`
- `#GTARP`
- `#007FirstLight`
- `#GamingShorts`
- `#ActionGaming`

Avoid:

- dialogue-heavy clips that start mid-sentence
- travel with no conflict
- misleading labels
- long cinematic setup without an immediate hook
