# Channel Strategy

Channel: MrLluminati Gaming

## Identity

MrLluminati Gaming is a gaming Shorts channel built around fast, readable moments from live gameplay and recorded sessions. The channel should feel like a place for quick chaos, clean reactions, funny mistakes, and tense survival/action moments.

## Current Positioning

The channel should not look completely random. Random uploads make it harder for YouTube and viewers to understand what the channel is about. A viewer who likes one Short should be able to predict that the next upload will have a similar energy.

Current strongest positioning:

- CS2 chaos and reaction Shorts as the growth engine.
- Survival horror / zombie games as a secondary pillar.
- Minecraft clips and cinematic/action moments as controlled experiments.

## Recommended Content Mix

- 70% CS2
- 20% survival horror / zombie games
- 10% Minecraft clips / cinematic experiments

This mix can change after analytics show a clear new winner. Until then, CS2 should get the most upload volume because toxic/random teammate content and solo queue chaos are currently the most promising directions.

## Why CS2 Leads

CS2 clips are easy to understand quickly. A viewer can see betrayal, timing, panic, or a kill payoff in the first second. That fits Shorts feed behavior better than slow story clips or long travel sequences.

## Secondary Pillars

Survival horror and zombie games can work when the payoff is immediate: a screamer, a horde, a jumpscare, a tunnel ambush, or low-ammo panic.

Minecraft clips can work as lightweight chaos clips. Use "Minecraft clips" unless the footage is clearly modded.

Cinematic/action games can work when the moment is visually clear without context: crashes, takedowns, train jumps, clean landings, and suspicious RP meetings.

## Upload Direction

Default to CS2 unless a non-CS2 clip has a stronger first frame and clearer payoff. The upload feed should feel focused enough that a new viewer understands the channel after 5-10 Shorts.
